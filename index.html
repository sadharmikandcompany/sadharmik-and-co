Our story

The meaning behind Sadharmik

साधर्मिक — those who walk the same path of dharma.

In our tradition, caring for a sadharmik — sharing food, faith and fellowship with those who follow the same values — is among the warmest acts of devotion. We named our khakhra after that spirit.

Every pack is made the way you would prepare food for your own family and faith: pure ingredients, no shortcuts, and real respect for what goes into your body. From the wheat we choose to the spices we roast, nothing enters our kitchen that wouldn't be welcome at a Jain table.

That is our promise — timeless, ethical and entirely yours.
